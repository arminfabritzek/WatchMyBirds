What gets exported?
Every explicit user interaction since the last batch — bundled into three buckets for Pipeline-Dev:
  • Hard-negatives — frames you marked "no bird" in moderation. Verified false-positive crops.
  • Confirmed positives — species you explicitly confirmed via Review-Queue, the Unclear "Confirm Day" action, or a bulk-relabel. Pipeline predictions you never touched are not in this bucket.
  • Species re-labels — corrections where you overrode the classifier's species pick.
  • Favorites — detections you heart-clicked in the gallery. The HUMAN gold-label, treated as highest-confidence training anchor.
Trash deletions and auto-tagged star ratings are deliberately excluded — they are ambiguous signals. Frames with mixed-status sibling detections are dropped from positive buckets to preserve training-correctness. Plan: 2026-05-22_FEATURE_user-groundtruth-export-for-pipeline-dev.
